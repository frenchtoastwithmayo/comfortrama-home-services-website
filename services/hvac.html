FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I service my furnace?

We recommend annual furnace maintenance before the heating season begins. This ensures optimal performance, energy efficiency, and helps prevent costly breakdowns during Ottawa's cold winters.

What size air conditioner do I need?

AC sizing depends on your home's square footage, insulation, windows, and other factors. Our technicians perform load calculations to determine the perfect size for maximum efficiency and comfort.

Do you offer emergency HVAC services?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency HVAC services throughout Ottawa. Whether your furnace fails on a cold winter night or your AC breaks during a heat wave, we're here to help.

Are there rebates available for new HVAC systems?

Yes, there are often federal and provincial rebates available for high-efficiency HVAC systems. We help you navigate available rebates and ensure you get maximum savings on your new system.
