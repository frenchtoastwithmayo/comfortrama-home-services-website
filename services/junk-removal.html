FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

What items can you remove?

We remove most household and commercial items including furniture, appliances, electronics, construction debris, and general clutter. We cannot remove hazardous materials, chemicals, or medical waste.

Do you provide same-day service?

Yes! We often provide same-day or next-day service depending on availability and location in Ottawa. Contact us early in the day for best same-day availability.

How do you determine pricing?

Pricing is based on volume (how much space items take in our truck), weight, and disposal requirements. We provide free, no-obligation estimates before starting any work.

Do you donate usable items?

We work with local Ottawa charities to donate items in good condition. This helps reduce waste and supports our community while potentially reducing your costs.
