FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

What constitutes a plumbing emergency?

Plumbing emergencies include burst pipes, severe leaks, sewer backups, no hot water, and any situation that could cause water damage or health hazards. We're available 24/7 for these urgent situations.

How quickly can you respond to emergency calls?

We aim to respond to emergency calls within 30 minutes and typically arrive on-site within 1-2 hours, depending on your location in Ottawa and current weather conditions.

Do you provide warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide warranties on all our plumbing work and the parts we install. The warranty period varies depending on the type of service, but we stand behind our quality workmanship.

Can you help with water heater selection?

We help you choose the right water heater based on your home's size, usage patterns, and energy efficiency goals. We install both traditional tank and tankless systems.
