FAQ Section

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to start landscaping projects?

Spring and early fall are ideal for most landscaping projects in Ottawa. Spring is perfect for planting and new installations, while fall is great for tree work and preparation for winter.

Do you provide landscape design services?

Yes! We offer complete landscape design services from initial consultation to final installation. Our designers work with you to create outdoor spaces that match your vision and budget.

How often should I have my lawn serviced?

Most Ottawa lawns benefit from weekly mowing during growing season (May-September) and bi-weekly during slower growth periods. We customize schedules based on your lawn's specific needs.

Do you offer snow removal services?

While our focus is on landscaping, we do offer seasonal property maintenance including light snow removal for our regular landscaping clients. Contact us for winter service options.
